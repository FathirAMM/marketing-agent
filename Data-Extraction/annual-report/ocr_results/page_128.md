# Management System

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[120, 240, 321, 495]]<|/det|>
Dilmah demonstrates its commitment to environmental stewardship through the adoption of the ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems (EMS) standard.The EMS framework which encompasses critical elements such as environmental planning, implementation and operation, monitoring and corrective actions, and regular management reviews, serves as the basis of managing the direct environmental impact resulting from the core business operations..

# GRI 301-1 MATERIALS

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[81, 566, 904, 607]]<|/det|>
Tea sourced from the Colombo auctions or directly from the network of tea smallholders around Sri Lanka, for production processes is the largest component of materials attributed to Dilmah's operations. Apart from Tea, the most significant material attributed to Dilmah's operations is packaging materials. In addition, the Company also procures herbs, flavouring agents and other essential items.

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[81, 621, 893, 632]]<|/det|>
A total of 92.5% of the materials used were derived from renewable sources, while only 7.5% were from non-renewable sources.
