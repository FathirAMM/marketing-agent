## GOOD GOVERNANCE, TRANSPARENCY AND BUSINESS ETHICS  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 793, 626, 852]]<|/det|>
Dilmah is a family business built on a legacy of good governance, transparency, and business ethics. The founder established a dynamic value system that is enshrined in the company's policies, standards, and operational manuals, ensuring the sustainability of the business for future generations.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 866, 611, 896]]<|/det|>
Dilmah has implemented robust Anti- Bribery and Corruption (ABC) Policy, Human Rights Policy, and Code of Ethics, fostering a strong sense of responsibility to remain  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 138, 902, 167]]<|/det|>
transparent and ethical in all aspects of the business.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 183, 916, 273]]<|/det|>
All of Dilmah's stakeholders—including the management team, employees, suppliers, and customers—are dedicated to upholding high standards of integrity, business ethics, transparency, and honesty in business operations.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 288, 896, 407]]<|/det|>
Performance monitoring measures and reporting systems are in place to ensure compliance with relevant policies, procedures, and controls. Continuous review of these policies for suitability, adequacy, and effectiveness ensures their ongoing relevance and comprehensiveness.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 423, 912, 483]]<|/det|>
Dilmah also provides a safe environment for reporting unethical behaviour and violations through established whistleblowing mechanisms.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 498, 914, 573]]<|/det|>
Dilmah was among the five companies ranked first in the Transparency in Corporate Reporting (TRAC) assessment conducted by Transparency International Sri Lanka (TISL) in 2023.

## SUSTAINABILITY AT THE HEART OF OUR OPERATIONS  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 620, 916, 860]]<|/det|>
The company is committed to creating enduring value by embedding sustainability at the core of its operations. The founder's vision of establishing a truly sustainable tea industry that benefits both people and the planet is deeply ingrained in Dilmah's ethos and organisational culture. The MJF Charitable Foundation (MJFCF) and Dilmah Conservation serve as the company's dedicated philanthropic and environmental branches. Primarily funded by the company, MJFCF and Dilmah Conservation undertake humanitarian and environmental sustainability initiatives across the island.
