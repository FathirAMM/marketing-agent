# STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 143, 335, 295]]<|/det|>
GRI 2- 29 At Dilmah, stakeholder engagement is viewed as a strategic lever for long- term value creation. Understanding the needs, concerns, and aspirations of stakeholders enables Dilmah to make informed decisions that strengthen resilience, enhance brand equity, and unlock new opportunities. These insights directly shape strategic priorities, from innovations, ethical

loyalty, in turn contributing to sustained financial performance and social and environmental impact.
