## GRI 2-9 BOARD COMPOSITION  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 843, 918, 902]]<|/det|>
Dilmah is governed by a well- structured and balanced Board of Directors that reflects the Company's commitment to strong corporate governance and ethical leadership. The composition of the Board is defined in accordance with the Company's Articles of Association and prevailing regulatory requirements, including those set forth under the Colombo Stock Exchange (CSE) Listing Rules on Corporate Governance.
