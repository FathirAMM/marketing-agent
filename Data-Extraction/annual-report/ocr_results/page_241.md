To operationalise this commitment, the Board also established a dedicated ESG governance structure, spearheaded by the ESG Board Committee. This Committee plays a leading role in providing strategic oversight, reviewing the alignment of sustainability goals with overall business strategy, and ensuring ESG priorities are meaningfully reflected in decision- making processes at the highest level. Among its key responsibilities, the Committee regularly reviews performance against ESG- related KPIs, monitors emerging sustainability trends and risks, and provides guidance on high- level decisions that shape the Company's long- term sustainability roadmap.
