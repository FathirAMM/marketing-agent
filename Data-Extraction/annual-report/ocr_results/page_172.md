# REPORT OF THE REMUNERATION COMMITTEE  

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[81, 140, 343, 153]]<|/det|>
## Committee Composition GRI 2-19, 2-20  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 156, 335, 304]]<|/det|>
The Remuneration Committee, appointed by the Board of Directors of Dilmah Ceylon Tea Company PLC, comprises of four Non- Executive Directors, of whom two are Independent as per the Listing Rules. Ms. Minette D A Perera stepped down from the Chair position on 09th October 2024 and Dr. Ravindra A. Fernando was nominated as the Chairman w.e.f.10th October 2024.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 320, 335, 424]]<|/det|>
Ms. Minette Perera and Mr. Rajan Asirwatham continue to serve as members of the Committee and Mr. John Lo was appointed as a member on 10th October 2024. The Secretary to the Board functions as the Secretary of the Committee.

## Role of the Committee  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 637, 323, 696]]<|/det|>
The Committee is responsible to the Board to determine the policy of the remuneration package of the Directors and Senior Management.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 712, 338, 786]]<|/det|>
The Committee evaluates, assesses and recommends to the Board of Directors on matters that may affect Human Resources Management of the Company and specifically include:  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 802, 334, 845]]<|/det|>
- Reviewing and providing recommendations to the Board on the Remuneration Policy of the Company  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[380, 140, 600, 168]]<|/det|>
and its specific application to the Directors and Senior Management.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[366, 184, 629, 243]]<|/det|>
- Reviewing the process of determination of the compensation of the Chief Executive, Executive Directors and Senior Management.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[366, 260, 623, 302]]<|/det|>
- Review the guidelines and parameters for annual salary increments and bonuses.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[366, 319, 619, 362]]<|/det|>
- Ensure that the necessary disclosures required in the Annual Report relating to director remuneration are made.  

<|ref|>sub_title<|/ref|><|det|>[[367, 378, 508, 391]]<|/det|>
## Remuneration Policy  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[366, 394, 623, 574]]<|/det|>
The remuneration policy of the Company is to attract, motivate and retain a highly qualified and experienced executive team, and reward performance accordingly in the backdrop of industry norms. These compensation packages provide compensation appropriate for each business within the Company and commensurate with each employee's level of expertise and contributions, bearing in mind the business' performance and shareholder returns.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[366, 590, 610, 648]]<|/det|>
The remuneration packages which are linked to individual performances are aligned with the Companies short term and long- term strategy.  

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[654, 140, 912, 199]]<|/det|>
Non- Executive Directors receive a fee for serving on the Board and serving on sub committees. They do not receive any performance related incentive payments.
