<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 455, 333, 584]]<|/det|>
**Principal Activities of the Group** The principal activity of the Group is to carry on the business of manufacture,export and marketing of value-added tea and cinnamon under the brand name "Dilmah". The principal activity of the Subsidiary Company is to manufacture,export and market tea in the form of liquid tea concentrate and ready-to-drink tea.

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 604, 342, 752]]<|/det|>
During the year under review the Company sort an amendment to its Objects clause as set out in the Articles of Association of the Company.Accordingly, on 01st April 2025, by approval of shareholder the Company adopted a new objects clause whereby its business activities were expanded into the areas of manufacture, export and marketing of value-added cinnamon.

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[80, 771, 330, 843]]<|/det|>
The Directors to the best of their knowledge and belief, confirm that the Company and the Group have not engaged in any activity that contravene laws and regulations.
