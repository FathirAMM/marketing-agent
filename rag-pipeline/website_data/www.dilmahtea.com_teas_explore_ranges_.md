Freshly harvested Ceylon tea leaves are transformed into a premium tea concentrate within hours at our state-of-the-art, ISO 9001:2015 & ISO 22000:2018 certified facility on Rilhena Estate. Our Natural Infusions celebrate the vibrant harmony of tea, herbs and spices, expertly crafted to deliver both exceptional flavour and natural wellness benefits. ## Arana Herbal Teas & Infusions

Arana — meaning “forest” in the Sinhala language — lies at the heart of our Ayurveda-inspired recipes, blending the pure goodness of Ceylon tea with healing herbs, roots, and spices sourced from Sri Lanka’s lush forests. Revered as Nature’s Ayurvedic pharmacy, these forests provide ingredients like Ashwagandha, Clove, Turmeric, Ceylon Cinnamon, Nutmeg, Soursop, and Gotukola, all celebrated for their health and wellness benefits. Hand-harvested by skilled artisans and carefully selected by Ayurvedic physicians, each ingredient in Arana teas and infusions is crafted under expert supervision to deliver authentic, natural wellbeing in every cup.
