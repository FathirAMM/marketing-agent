# Capital Management

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[128, 157, 903, 182]]<|/det|>
The Company manages its capital to ensure that it will be able to continue as a going concern while maximising the return to shareholders through the optimisation of the debt and equity balance.

<|ref|>text<|/ref|><|det|>[[128, 197, 915, 236]]<|/det|>
The Company makes adjustments to its capital structure, in light of changes in economic and business conditions. To maintain or adjust the capital structure, the Company may issue new shares or adjust dividend payments to shareholders. No changes were made in the objectives, policies or processes during the year ended 31 March 2025 and 2024.
